CHAPTER: 02

'AI' Applications

  1. Robotics
  2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  3. Computer Vision

Robotics

Robotics is closely related to AI, focusing on the design, construction, and operation of robots. AI-powered robots use sensors, actuators, and decision-making algorithms to interact with their environment and perform tasks autonomously.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP is a branch of AI that focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. It encompasses tasks such as language translation, sentiment analysis, chatbots, and virtual assistants.

Computer Vision

Computer vision is the field of AI that deals with enabling machines to interpret and understand the visual world. It involves tasks such as object detection, image classification, facial recognition, and video analysis.
